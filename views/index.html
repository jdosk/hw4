What is data?

Data is basically any and all information worth storing. It can be as simple as numbers or names or even letters.

What is a database?

A database is a system used to collect and store data. Once stored, the data can be organized or manipulated to the desire of anyone with access. Databases allow for the massive organization and processing of information, and have allowed for the great advances in technology and computing we see today.
